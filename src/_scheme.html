Scheme of working with us

Request
You leave a request on our website or call us
Delivery analytics
We calculate delivery options for your shipment
Contract
We conclude a contract with you for the delivery of goods from China
Delivery to our warehouse
We contact your supplier in China and arrange delivery to our warehouse
Cargo control
We check, assemble and pack your cargo in our warehouse before shipping
Cargo delivery
We transport your cargo to its destination and carry out customs clearance
Payment
You pay for your shipment when you receive it
